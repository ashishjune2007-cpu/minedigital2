Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

How long does a typical website project take?

Most websites are delivered within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. E-commerce sites or custom web applications may take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our strategy call.

Do you offer monthly retainers or just one-time projects?

We offer both! Our Starter package is project-based, while Growth and Enterprise are monthly retainers that include ongoing optimization, support, and marketing management.

What makes MineDigital different from other agencies?

We're a premium boutique agency focused on quality over quantity. You get direct access to senior strategists, custom solutions (no templates), and a guarantee of ROI-positive campaigns or we work for free.

Do you work with clients outside Delhi NCR?

Absolutely! While we're based in Greater Noida and love supporting local Delhi NCR businesses, we work with clients across India and internationally. Video calls make collaboration seamless.
